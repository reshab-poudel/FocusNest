FocusNest

A calm productivity space for planning tasks, focusing deeply, and capturing ideas.

Abilities

  • Create, complete, and remove tasks quickly
  • Run a distraction-free focus timer
  • Save notes instantly in your browser

Advantages

  • Works fast with no sign-up required
  • Keeps your data locally for privacy
  • Simple interface helps reduce mental load

How It Works

  1. Add tasks and mark progress as you finish.
  2. Set timer minutes and press Start for focus mode.
  3. When time ends, a soft 30-second alarm plays.
  4. Use notes to track thoughts or next steps.
